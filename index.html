Average Temperature in September Per State for Years 2013 - 2020

Temperature changes year to year are a big topic of conversation in the U.S.. Are they rising or falling on average? The symbols outlined in red represent states with a higher average temperature for the month of September.

The states with a lower average temperature in September are displayed in blue.

All temperature data is determined by The National Centers for Environmental Information.
